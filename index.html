Tender Chicken Rice & Crispy Siu Yuk!

Restaurant:Restaurant Kar Heong

Location:SS14 Subang Jaya

Price: below RM20

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Tender chicken, Crispy Siu Yuk, Crunchy Taugeh and fragrant rice. One of the best Chicken rice with Siu Yok I've tried.
